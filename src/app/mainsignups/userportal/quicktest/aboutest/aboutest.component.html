About Tests
Our Quick test is a combination of two mental health tests that are scientifically proven to for detecting Anxiety and Depression The First test is GAD-7 (Generalized Anxiety Disorder) The Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-Item Scale (GAD-7) is a self-report questionnaire that helps identify and assess generalized anxiety disorder The Second test is PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire) The Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9) is a brief, 9-item screening tool used to assess the severity of depression and other mental health challenges.
