Our Mission

At MindMentor, we believe mental health care should be accessible, accurate, and personalized. Our platform bridges the gap between individuals struggling with mental health challenges and licensed professionals who can help.

Using scientifically validated assessment tools like GAD-7 for anxiety and PHQ-9 for depression, combined with advanced sentiment analysis of social media activity, we provide a comprehensive initial screening that helps identify those in need of support.

How It Works

Scientific Assessment

Our system uses clinically validated GAD-7 and PHQ-9 questionnaires to screen for anxiety and depression with medical-grade accuracy.

Sentiment Analysis

Advanced AI analyzes language patterns in your social media to detect early warning signs of mental health challenges.

Personalized Matching

When needed, we connect you with licensed therapists who are the best fit for your specific needs and preferences.

Seamless Booking

Our integrated scheduling system makes booking appointments with mental health professionals simple and stress-free.

Our Platform

MindMentor offers three specialized portals to serve all stakeholders in the mental health care process:

Patient Portal

Take assessments, view results, book appointments, and track your mental health journey with our user-friendly interface.

Therapist Portal

Manage appointments, view patient assessments (with consent), and provide care through our secure professional dashboard.

Admin Portal

Oversee platform operations, verify therapist credentials, and ensure the highest standards of care are maintained.
